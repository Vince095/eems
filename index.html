About the School

The school was founded by Mr. Limema Phohlo in 1996 where he started with pre-school and primary. Later in 2014, high school was founded from Form A. From there , Form B up to Form E were included in 2015. The scool is now composed of pre-school lower primary, upper primary, secondary and high school. The students' performance is quite encouraging because teachers play a major role to see to it that instructions in the classrooms cater for every student including those with special needs and on the other hand learners show a great concern for their studies. The school is currently in the process to be registered as an international school.

Home Away from Home

Residential life is an integral part of the school. Boarders grow to be self-reliant individuals and develop independent living skills in stimulating environment at Emmanuel English Medium School. They learn to identify their needs co-operate with each other and take responsibility of their own decisions. School offers hostel facilities for girls and boys. The hostels are looked after by the wardens and the Principal is in direct contact with every student.
